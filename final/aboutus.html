About Us

Our Mission

Our mission has always been to share the experience of nature, and Pacific Trails has always been a great setting to foster it. Whether it is hiking, kayaking, or relaxing in the yurts, our goal is to provide both a serene and an exciting experience at Pacific Trails.

Our Inspiration

As a family of five, hiking has always been our primary way of bonding. Whether it was enjoying the scenery or watching the sunrise, we have always shared a fascination whenever we set out on an expedition. We hope to share these same experiences with others through Pacific Trails Resort.

Feedback from Our Customers

Since the founding of Pacific Trails Resort, we have received immense, positive feedback. Some of this feedback has guided the additions made to Pacific Trails Resort over the course of its existence, such as the addition of yoga sessions.

Our Plans for The Future

In light of the feedback we will continue to receive, we are planning the addition of new activities. As a long-term goal of ours, we hope to build an even richer and more exciting experience at Pacific Trails Resort!
